Tippie Top 21

The University of Iowa: Tippie College of Business

May 2024

The Tippie Top 21 award honors 21 exceptional student leaders at The University of Iowa's Tippie College of Business who have excelled in scholarship, leadership, experiential learning, and advancing diversity, inclusion, and global engagement.

Mental Health Champion

The University of Iowa

April 2024

The Mental Health Champion Award at The University of Iowa recognizes individuals who make significant and sustainable contributions to campus mental health. It supports the university's strategic goal of "Holistic Well-Being and Success," which embeds mental health into the campus culture. Awardees are those who attend to their own mental health, center diversity, equity, accessibility, and inclusivity, and improve access to and awareness of mental health resources.

Dean's List

The University of Iowa

May 2021, May 2022, August 2023, December 2023

The Dean's List is awarded every semester to students who demonstrate high academic performance by achieving a semester GPA of 3.5 or above, with a minimum of 12 semester hours. This recognition celebrates and acknowledges the hard work and dedication of students excelling in their academic pursuits.
