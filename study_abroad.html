Winter Study Abroad Program

Council on International Educational Exchange (CIEE)

December 2023 to January 2024

I had the opportunity to participate in the International Business in London, Data Analytics course. During my two-week stay in London, England, I worked closely with my team to research and compile a comprehensive report and presentation. The class examined the business strategy employed by Ryan Reynolds and Rob McElhenney in their acquisition of Wrexham AFC, focusing on the resulting economic impact. My group specifically concentrated on identifying the risks that were mitigated and examining new ones that emerged as a result of the acquisition.

Term GPA: 4.0/4.0
